NewViewNews.com

NewViewNews.com is a website I programmed from scratch meant to be a place where opinions from all sides can be heard and discussed. The concept depends on users to write their own articles that can then be responded to and "spun-off" to provide all the perspectives on different matters; making sure that partisan-bias does not sway opinions. The site is still a work-in-progress but shows a lot of potential.
